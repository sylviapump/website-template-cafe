Template Cafe is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of the city. You can't miss the aroma of artisanal coffee beans being freshly ground as you approach. We offer a diverse all-day breakfast as well as a lunch menu that emphasizes fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and a touch of innovation. Come and see us at Template Café 123 Main Street, Launceston.

Alex & Jun

The owners of Template Café, Alex and Jun, are passionate visionaries with a deep love for culinary arts and community engagement. With backgrounds in hospitality and technology, they dreamed of creating a space that seamlessly blends tradition with innovation. Their shared vision brought Template Café to life in 2018 as a vibrant hub where customers can enjoy exceptional food, personalized service, and unique themed experiences. Alex's expertise in sustainable practices and Jun's flair for creative menu design have set a new standard for café culture in the city, making Template Café a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike.
